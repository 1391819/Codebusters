QA Cinemas

QA Cinemas is an industry-recognized cinema chain that has been in the cinema and film business for over 20 years. We specialize in both new releases and special showings of classic films from the 60's until the 90's. We have seen large success in the Northwest of England, and we plan on branching out nationally within the next coming years.
